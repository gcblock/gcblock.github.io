# Design and implementation of algorithm based on entrustment and guarantee behavior

# Pick to

  At present, when people enjoy digital products on the Internet, they are faced with huge trust cost. The natural decentralization of blockchain technology, data cannot be tampered with, traceability and other characteristics are efficient solutions to solve the problem of Internet trust and improve production efficiency. However, the consensus algorithm adopted by the current blockchain technology still has problems such as weak decentralization, low throughput and waste of resources. With the vigorous development of the Internet of Things and artificial intelligence, enterprises and governments are in urgent need of developing the blockchain technology, so as to realize the building of rich upper application and widely recognized blockchain technology.

  By studying consensus algorithms such as PoW and DPOS, it is found that they all have resource-oriented security shortcomings. When resources are centralized, it means that a single node has a high weight in the public, and the imtamperable property of blockchain data will be invalid.Entrust the security Certificate consensus algorithm (Guarantee Certificate) referred to as "GC, inspired by method of the astronomical Taoism, commissioned in the society and guarantee the behavior of the chain of responsibility mechanism is constructed to guarantee the data cannot be tampered with, the client pay the bills, the guarantor guarantees the bill is valid, the top node honest record client submit payment chain provide the basis for yourself, the final data aggregation for payer bill is" inevitable ", legal if not legally initiate collective arbitration, restriction and accountability for cheaters.Through this responsibility chain mechanism, multi-direction supervision is formed in the community to monitor cheating behavior in the blockchain network. User resources are guaranteed through the accountability mechanism, cheaters are punished, and attacks bear huge risks. The security capability is not dependent on the proportion of individual resources, and resource centralization is refused.The GC consensus algorithm takes the role as the unit of the system, so that a single node can be deployed in a cluster, and the load capacity of individual nodes can be improved horizontally.Bills generated by all nodes on the data are valid data and constitute a responsibility chain to realize value drive. The payment mode of TPS with low risk and "infinite high" (only considering the dimensions of consensus, not considering the limitations of hardware and underlying system dependence) and the payment mode with zero risk waiting for aggregation confirmation are selected flexibly for risk assessment.In this paper, the algorithm process will be described in detail around the above functional points and the flow chart. Based on this theoretical basis, the blockchain engine based on the chain of responsibility mechanism will be realized. Finally, whether it meets the theoretical design requirements will be verified through functional tests and performance tests.

  The block chain system developed based on GC consensus algorithm theory can realize horizontal expansion in throughput capacity after testing. It is driven by value and does not produce waste data and does not waste resources. As the underlying database, it can provide on-chain solutions for N types of industries, build rich block chain applications and improve the efficiency of multi-industries.

# Chapter 2: GC consensus algorithm

  This chapter describes GC consensus algorithm through the chain of responsibility to implement the monitoring mechanism, arbitration mechanism, to realize the decentralization of blockchain, data can not be tamper with the characteristics.

# 2.1 Consensus Unit

  GC Consensus Unit is divided into four categories: Client, Guarantor, Top Node and Aggregate Node.Each consensus unit is responsible for different algorithms, so that each node can supervise each other and ensure that the overall data is honest and reliable and can not be tampered with.

# 2.1.1 the principal

  The client is the producer of the data, and the data signed by the client can only be recognized by the GC blockchain system after being guaranteed by the guarantor, so the client must have a fixed guarantor to cooperate with the normal business exhibition. In this paper, the guarantor of the client is described as "the account holder".Client can not arbitrarily transfer accounts, transfer application need to freeze after a certain period to take effect, this time setting is flexible, at the top of the real-name system of chain blocks or node has a very high credibility, such as the functions of the government units, can shorten the freeze time, or even cancel the freezing time. The freeze time considerations are based on the system security Settings, and the release of the freeze depends on the completion of the aggregation node execution.

# 2.1.2 the guarantor

  The name of guarantor means that the data produced by the guarantor is true and effective and has not been tampered with.The trustor can freeze the assets of the account and make a public statement as the guarantor to provide guarantee services. The guarantee ability depends on the quantity of the frozen assets.When a guarantor receives a large transaction from the principal that exceeds its guarantee capacity, it can choose other guarantor nodes to form a joint guarantee.Acceptance of the offer of guarantee by other guarantors is not mandatory. It is customized by individual terminals. For example, it can be set to accept a certain guarantor, or it must be signed and approved by top nodes to form joint guarantee, etc.

# 2.1.3 Top Node

  The top node selects the node with the most guarantee ability in the whole network, and the guarantor role is responsible for it, but it is only responsible for honestly recording the sequence of the payment chain sent by all the guarantor nodes, and providing inquiry service to other guarantors.The top node is an important basis for the receiver node to judge whether the limit of the guarantor is overspent and whether the payment chain is legal or not.

  When a blockchain is initialized, the top node is the first block. The validity of the signature data of the top node is monitored by the way of periodic "meeting communication" of the nodes. If the top node cheating, other nodes will abrogate and re-select the top node by proof.

# 2.1.4 Aggregate nodes

  Aggregate nodes are the nodes used to aggregate payment chains and issue arbitration claims.When the payment chains are aggregated, cheating nodes will show "overspend" conflicts. When the node obtains the conflicting payment chains, it will initiate arbitration and deduct the guarantor deposit to fill the overspend bill.If the top node is found cheating, the deposit of the top node will be deducted and its account will be frozen, and then the node with the most guarantee ability will be re-selected as the top node.If there is no cheating, all payment chains will be signed by the guarantor as an unalterable fact.

  Aggregation nodes are the ultimate safeguard against cheating, and the frequency with which they are executed is a key parameter for achieving a public ledger that is close to completely reliable.

Therefore, due to the existence of supervision, the real-name blockchain system can improve the transaction efficiency without waiting for the completion of aggregation to confirm the transaction, because individuals who overspend will be recovered, and units who overspend will be held accountable. # 2.3 Arbitration mechanism to achieve reliable blockchain

  The arbitration mechanism of GC consensus algorithm can avoid the double payment loophole.Theoretically any node in the liability chain may "mutiny", but the arbitration mechanism can protect the rights and interests of the payee.When the most secure top node "defections" will cause the "payer" to pay more bills than it can afford.In the case that the total assets of the blockchain remain unchanged and the non-real name system means that the payee will lose interests. Arbitration mechanism oriented results have three kinds: the abandonment of the transaction, the payee 100 percent loss; If the arbitration is successful, the payee will not lose. Arbitration is successful, but after deducting the liability of the guarantee money is still not enough to fill up the cheating part of this transaction.

  The cost of cheating is extremely high, and whether or not the cheating is successful, when the cheating node generates cheating data that is detected and arbitrated by other nodes means the risk of losing the deposit and freezing the account. In the real name system, there is even legal liability.

# 2.3.1 "Mutiny" of the Payor's Guarantor

  A "defection" by a payer's guarantor is when a payer's bill overruns, but the payer's account guarantor signs the payment chain to trick the payee into accepting the transaction by hiding the disbursement or falsifying the income.

  This cheating only makes sense if the overspend is greater than the guarantee plus the payer's balance.Otherwise, the arbitration mechanism will not only deduct the payer's balance and guarantee amount, but also lock up the account.

  It is very risky for the payee to accept this bill without verifying the balance guaranteed by the payer at the top node.If the other party cheats, the unfilled part of the arbitration mechanism will be lost.The reason why GC consensus algorithm does not enforce the verification of top-level nodes is to give a more flexible experience to the payment network and realize the "check function". Whether the final check is valid depends on the reality.

# 2.3.2 "Mutiny" of Top Node

  By providing false pay top node chain sequence the payer guarantor pay nodes have the ability to appear to be legitimate, but because the payee is the top node credit payer's important basis, so the top node cheating data will conflict with block chain network with a node in the data, found cheating behavior is one of the important parameters to avoid loss of time.

  Again, cheating only makes sense if the overspend is greater than the guarantee amount + the payers' balance + the top node's guarantee amount.Otherwise, the arbitration mechanism will not only deduct the payer's balance and guarantee amount, but also lock the account of the top node, and then aggregate the data of the whole network to select the new node with the highest guarantee amount.

# 3.4.1 Asymmetric encryption algorithm

  Asymmetric encryption algorithms require two keys: the PUBLICKEY and the PRIVATEKEY.https://baike.baidu.com/item/公开密钥/7453570 (opens new window)A public key is paired with a private key. If the public key is used to encrypt data, only the corresponding private key can be used to decrypt it. Because encryption and decryption use two different keys, this algorithm is called asymmetric encryption algorithm. The basic process of the asymmetric encryption algorithm to realize the exchange of confidential information is as follows: Party A generates a pair of keys and discloses the public key, and other roles (Party B) who need to send information to Party A encrypt the confidential information using the key (Party A's public key) before sending it to Party A;https://baike.baidu.com/item/密钥/101144 (opens new window)Party A then decrypts the encrypted information with its own private key. On the contrary, when Party A wants to reply Party B, it encrypts the data using Party B's public key, and similarly, Party B uses its own private key to decrypt the data.

  On the other hand, Party A may use its private key to sign the Confidential Information before sending it to Party B; Party B shall use Party A's public key to check the signature of the data sent back by Party A.

# 3.4.2 Vertex selection algorithm

  GC chain stipulates that the guarantor node with the highest guarantee amount and no breach of trust in the data set is the top node, referred to as the apex.If the top node cannot provide service due to network or hardware reasons, the top node shall be re-elected. If the service is restored, the top node can apply for returning to the top node.

Cannot serve conditions for electing other top nodes:

  No result returned within 10 minutes is recorded as one failure. If the number of failures of the same node is more than 6 times within an hour, the report can be reported in the next aggregation process, and the weight of the report is the processing ability.If the cumulative weight of the report exceeds half of the total weight in the whole network, the report is successful and the top node is re-elected.

# 3.4.3 Aggregation node selection algorithm

  Each node can initiate an aggregation request, but this behavior needs to be controlled so that the entire system does not become overloaded frequently.

(1) Don't reject data you care about

  From the analysis of the aggregate demand, the aggregate data is to monitor whether there is cheating at the top nodes, while the guarantor is actually more concerned about the data of the bill principals with their own economy. So a single node in the GC chain that initiates an aggregation request must first publish the list of interested clients.When other nodes in the system receive an aggregation request from a delegate of common interest, they "cannot refuse (there is no reason to refuse)" to communicate all the data.

(2) Do not reject the concerned data, and choose the node with the "strongest aggregation ability" as the center for data delivery

  In order to "nip it in the bud," each node tends to want to own the entire bill. But assuming that the system has too many nodes, or that a hacker causes too many nodes to initiate an aggregation request, satisfying the aggregation request of all the nodes means that the load on each node in the system is extremely high and wasteful.

  In order to solve this problem, we require the node to not only publish the relevant data of the client that it cares about, but also publish the data aggregation of its guarantee when it initiates the aggregation request. The system sets a certain time interval to consider the aggregation complete.

  Because the "care" cascade exists, it means that the aggregation request is made and the number of aggregation nodes that you care about varies.Here, the node with the largest number of aggregation nodes is selected as the node with the "strongest aggregation capability".

  1. Data security

  Since the ability to aggregate is strongest and predictable, will the nodes with the strongest ability to aggregate be turned against? This is a problem that can be solved 100 percent. A block of data aggregated from any node has the signature of that node. If tampered, it will be "noticed" by the node that synchronizes the data at the first time, and the node will be added to the blacklist. When the data is aggregated again, the blacklisted node will be permanently removed from the aggregate node.

  1. Be responsible for relevant data

  When the aggregation is initiated, no matter whether other nodes ask or actively participate in the aggregation, they shall submit honest payment chain data guaranteed by themselves. If they are not honest, the part hidden by themselves will not be accepted by the public, and the part contradictory with other nodes will be held responsible and punished.Therefore, each node will actively and honestly publish its own data.

  Guarantee service is divided into two parts, one is to receive the bill signature of the client, the other is to accept the payment chain of other guarantors to form a joint guarantee.The second guaranteed node can be optionally turned on or off.

  Calculate the balance of the client, and sign and guarantee the bill when the balance is sufficient.

Accepts payment chains from the principal sent by other guarantors:

  According to the parameter setting of the node: simply check the payment chain of the client and judge the sufficient balance to sign and guarantee; Guarantee is signed by the top node after signature; The data is aggregated and then signed for guarantee. Risk levels go down sequentially, but so does throughput efficiency.

# 3.5.4 Monitoring module

  After the user's blockchain wallet receives the payment, it will check whether the payer's payment chain has overspent. Overspending means that the payer and the guarantor are cheating. If the top-level node signs, it means that the top-level node is also cheating. At this time, the cheating information can be submitted to the arbitration module for processing.

After aggregating the data, the Guarantor will check whether the payment chain including the Guarantor node and the Guarantor node opening an account at the current node is overspent, and then do the same as above.

# 3.5.5 Arbitration module

  The mediation transaction will begin immediately after receiving the mediation initiated by the monitor module.

Guarantor cheats:

  Guarantor cheating means that data does not pass the signature, the top node at this time only need to issue the bill submitted to the arbitration module at the top of the node to recover losses, the top node after receiving problem bill will be deducted after confirm true bills the decision of the security deposit, other nodes synchronize data synchronization when the decision and approval. Unfilled parts will be lost.

Top Node Cheating:

  The top node cheating by polymerization of the transaction, the principal or the guarantor after submit the top node cheating evidence, all nodes in the same rules when decide to deduct the bill sponsor deposit, if lack of bill amount will be added to the top node deposit, the abolition of the top node at the same time, according to the vertices selection algorithm to select the new node at the top.Unfilled parts will be lost.
