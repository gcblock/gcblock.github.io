# Watermelon: block chain

# The introduction

Blockchain is a term used in information technology.In essence, it is a shared database, the data or information stored in it, has the characteristics of "non-forgeable", "whole-process trace", "traceable", "open and transparent", "collective maintenance", etc.Based on these characteristics, blockchain technology has laid a solid foundation of "trust" and created a reliable mechanism of "cooperation", which has a broad application prospect.

On January 10, 2019, the Cyberspace Administration of China released the "Blockchain Information Service Management Regulations" [1]On October 24, 2019, in the 18th group study of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee, General Secretary Xi Jinping stressed that "blockchain is an important breakthrough in the independent innovation of core technology" and "to accelerate the innovation and development of blockchain technology and industry"."Blockchain" has entered the public vision and become the focus of social attention.

On Dec 2, 2019, the word was selected as one of the top 10 buzzwords of 2019 by Yaowen Qiaozi. ---- excerpted from Baidu Baike

# Blockchain and consensus algorithms

Consensus algorithm can be seen to realize the core algorithm of blockchain technology. The core properties of blockchain are non-forgeable, whole-process trace, traceable (query), open and transparent, and collective maintenance are completed by "consensus".How to do, big guy (distributed) terminal together negotiations to reach a common implementation of the articles of association (this is the origin of the consensus), the articles of association (algorithm) first data store open and transparent, anyone can query, someone tried to forge the big guy does not recognise the data, and banned, can even set the punishment mechanism. How to achieve the specific fairies have their own methods, such as PoW, PoS...Both have advantages and disadvantages.
