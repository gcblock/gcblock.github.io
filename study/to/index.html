# Digital operating units

Blockchain technology can solve the problems of cheating and corruption, so it is necessary for digital operating units to provide a fair and non-supervisory investment operating environment.

# Overview of the Digital Approach

The 100% quantified equity of the company is the gold custody contract. The assets marked in the contract are gold, and the basic unit is G, that is, one share is equal to 1 G gold. Shareholders can freely choose to enter or withdraw the share capital through the block chain system. Shareholder rights are 100% based on blockchain system custody. The quantified digital assets include share capital, power, public welfare, namely holding gold custody contract is unit shareholder.

# equity

Capital stock is the condition for investors to enter, and the basic investment unit is gold G. Gold is chosen not only because it has thousands of years of circulation history and wide acceptance, but also because it can ensure investors' property is not disturbed by the off-site environment to the greatest extent, so that investors can advance and retreat in any environment at any time.

# equity

Shareholder rights are divided into dividends and voting rights. In order to support the financing development of the company, except for the privilege dividend part, the remaining rights of each shareholder are equal.

# ⭐️ privilege dividend

In order to finance the development of the unit and encourage the upper class to rush in, the company stipulates the privilege dividend method. The longest time of the privilege dividend is not more than 9 years, and it cannot be renewed for longer than that time. It is converted into the share dividend, and the access conditions are decided by the leadership of the unit.

# ⭐️ Uni-share Dividend

Subtract the share of the dividend in the privileged shares, the remaining dividend is divided equally among the shareholders.

# ⭐️ Share Share Vote

No matter how many gold custody contracts each shareholder holds, its voting power is the same, that is, equal voting. The results of the vote are generated through the blockchain, which is a blockchain developed using the blockchain consensus algorithm and is the blockchain for the storage of the gold custody contract.

# ⭐️ Angel investing uses a privileged dividend scheme

In order to better financing development, when the consortium in the way of financing to enter the privileged dividend power, the privileged shares enjoy the number of contracts held by the score of red.

# ⭐️ VIP treatment

Company shareholders enjoy VIP treatment when enjoying company resources. The formulation of VIP rules is determined by the equal-rights shareholders voting through the block consensus chain. In the event of productivity spillover or natural disaster, VIP treatment can make shareholders reach a consensus on the allocation of resources, and ultimately provide benefits for shareholders or reasonably allocate resources.On the other hand, in the normal development planning of the company, VIP treatment can play an incentive role in the development of the company.

# 🔖 Patent Policy

An open patent policy can truly provide benefits to inventors, rather than make a patent a capitalist windfall weight.

There is no monopoly in the patents generated by the company, and there is a unified calculation rule: patent contributors enjoy 20% of the annual total profits as economic income, and the period of enjoyment is judged by the legal patent agency.There are no other rights.

# General manager's rights

The general manager is the highest position of the company, with the power of personnel appointment and removal, investment and operation.

The general manager is appointed and removed at the annual general meeting of shareholders. When the contract holder believes that the general manager's behavior is harmful to the company, he or she can limit or remove the general manager's rights through blockchain voting.

When the general manager's operating activities invoke funds that exceed 30% of the company's total assets, it is necessary to raise the block chain for 3 days to vote. When the negative votes exceed 1%, the voting time is extended by 12 days.The final decision is whether the yes vote outweighs the no vote.
